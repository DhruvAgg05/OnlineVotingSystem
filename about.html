SEHEJ-MATH

SEHEJ-MAT is a software platform that helps you make important decisions with your group. Whether you need to elect leaders, vote on policies, or collect feedback, SEHEJ-MAT lets you do it online, securely and efficiently. With SEHEJ-MAT, you can create ballots, invite voters, monitor participation, and view results in real time. SEHEJ-MAT is the ideal online voting solution for organizations of any size and type.

Elections

Conducting governmental, organizational, or community elections with remote accessibility.

Referendums/Polls

Gathering opinions on issues or decisions through surveys and referendums.

Board/AGM Voting

Facilitating remote voting during board meetings or annual general meetings for shareholders or members.

Student Council Elections

Engaging students in democratic processes by allowing them to vote remotely for their representatives.
